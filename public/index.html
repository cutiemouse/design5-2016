Talent is Cheap. Dedication is costly.

Debbie Jiang is based in Vancouver, holding a doctorate degree of Fabric Materials Design from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University.

Due to her talent, love and passion for design, after moving to Vancouver, she decided to take graphic design as her lifetime career and thus received a systematic training at AI (Art Institute of Vancouver), where she produced the strongest portfolio among 2006 Graphic Design students.

She has been working as an Interactive Designer & Frontend Developer at Canada Wide Media Limited, western Canada's largest independent multi-media publisher, since 2007. Debbie has a keen eye for design trend, passionate about new web developing technologies and always managed to produce crisp, trendy and creative designs for clients.

In 2009, one of her design work BCBusiness website took first place for Best Website Design in New Media BC PopVox Awards against some tough contenders, including Lululemon, the Interior Designers Institute of B.C., and Vitamin Daily.
